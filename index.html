The Pomodoro Technique

consists in a Time Management technique that increase your productivity based on job focus period -25min- interspersed with short rest period -5min-

but... if you are very tired, you can opt for a longer rest

The Six Steps of Pomodoro

1-Choose a task you'd like to complete

2-Set the Pomodoro Timer (25:00)

3- Once you start, be fully focused until time runs out

4- When the Pomodoro rings a alert, your first task is done!

5- A SHORT BREAK... [5:00]

6- Every 4 Pomodoros, take a longer break [15:00]
